About Me

Get to know more about who I am, what I do, and my passion for software development.

Hi, I'm Ujjaval

I am a dedicated and passionate software developer with a strong background in Computer Engineering. With a Bachelor of Engineering degree from L.J Institute of Engineering and Technology, I have honed my skills in various technologies such as JavaScript, Angular, Leaflet, Google Maps API, and more. My focus is on building efficient and scalable web applications with an emphasis on user-friendly interfaces and performance optimization.

Whether I’m creating dynamic, responsive websites or developing APIs, I thrive on problem-solving and continually learning new technologies to improve my craft. My portfolio showcases a range of projects where I have applied my knowledge to real-world challenges, delivering solutions that make an impact.

Outside of coding, I’m enthusiastic about contributing to the tech community, staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends, and collaborating with like-minded individuals to push the boundaries of software development.
